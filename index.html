Bio

Peter was born and raised in Montclair, NJ. Growing up he played football, hockey and lacrosse, and was also and avid snowboarder and climber. Peter served in the US Navy completing the Basic Reconnaissance Course, Army Airborne, Marine Combatant Dive, Diving Medicine, High Altitude Medicine, Mountain Warfare, Dynamic Assault, Advanced Trauma, among many other schools to serve as a Reconnaissance Corpsman with the 1st Reconnaissance Bn, and Batallion Landing Team 3/1 USMC. After his service he moved on to become a Paramedic, and worked across the country in hospital and field care. Peter later moved on to web development and has excelled in the field ever since.
